<p>The was Gogo's first experience with post-modern decorating and she was clearly tantalized by it. As soon as she got into the room, she started running around like a crazy person. I don't know if it had anything to do with the fact that the comforter was down, but when she jumped on the bed, she crouched down and started chewing aimlessly. Go figure.</p>
